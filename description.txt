3 text files containing tab-delimited log2 expression ratios for 3924 genes under 1 of 3 conditions compared to an appropriate control sample.  Files have genes in rows and experiments in columns.  

iron.txt
cellwall.txt
translation.txt


1 text file with gene IDs in row order matching the 3 expression data files above.

rowNames.txt


Expression data is a subset of the data described in the following paper:
Boshoff et al. (2004)  "The Transcriptional Responses of Mycobacterium tuberculosis to Inhibitors of Metabolism."   Journal of Biological Chemistry.  279(38):40174-40184.
